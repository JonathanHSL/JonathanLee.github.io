Some facts about Lifehouse

Lifehouse is an American rock band, originating from Los Angeles. The band includes Jason Wade (lead vocals), Rick Woolstenhulme, Jr. (drums), and Bryce Soderberg (bass).

The band came to be more recognized through their single "Hanging by a Moment" which was number one for three weeks on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart and made it tonumber 2 on the Billboard Hot 100 charts.

Some of their songs seem to have spiritual influence, but their lyrics are open to self interpretation.
